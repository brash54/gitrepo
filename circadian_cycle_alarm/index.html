• The app will sound an alarm at sunrise on the choosen date.
  • Enter your 5 digit zipcode and pick the date you want the alarm to sound.
  • The app relies on your PC's settings to determine local time.
  • To test the app, reset your PC's local time to a few minutes before sunrise time.
  • Replace the mp3 file in the sound folder to change the alarm sound.

The app uses the Sunrise-Sunset.org API, a Google Maps Platform API, and FlipClock.js
